Production method:
           The cooking techniques of Anhui Cuisine cuisine include knife work, heat and operation techniques. The most important thing of Anhui Cuisine cuisine is fire work, which has always been a fine tradition. Its uniqueness is mainly reflected in the Kung Fu dishes that are good at cooking, stewing, fumigation and steaming. The use of different fire control techniques for different dishes is an important symbol of Anhui Cuisine chefs' proficiency, and also a basic means to form a unique style of crisp, tender, fragrant and fresh. There are about 20 categories and more than 50 kinds of cooking techniques commonly used in Anhui Cuisine cuisine, among which the most representative ones are smooth cooking, braising, clear stewing and raw fumigation.

制作方法： 徽菜的烹饪技法，包括刀工、火候和操作技术，徽菜之重火工是历来的优良传统，其独到之处集中体现为擅长烧、炖、熏、蒸类的功夫菜上，不同菜肴使用不同的控火技术是徽帮厨师造诣深浅的重要标志，也是徽菜能形成酥、嫩、香、鲜独特风格的基本手段，徽菜常用的烹饪技法约有20大类50余种，其中最能体现徽式特色的是滑烧、红烧、清炖和生熏法。
