Introduction:
         Zhejiang cuisine, abbreviated as Zhejiang cuisine, is one of the eight major cuisines of the Han ethnic group in China. With its picturesque scenery and abundant resources, there is a saying: "Heaven above, Suzhou and Hangzhou below.".

简介： 浙江菜，简称浙菜，是中国汉族八大菜系之一 ，其地山清水秀，物产丰富，故谚曰：“上有天堂，下有苏杭”。

Zhejiang cuisine features:
        Zhejiang cuisine, based on the four major schools mentioned above, has distinct distinctive styles and shares four common characteristics: careful selection of ingredients, unique cooking, emphasis on the original flavor, and meticulous preparation.

浙菜特色： 浙菜基于以上四大流派，就整体而言，有比较明显的特色风格，又具有共同的四个特点：选料讲究，烹饪独到，注重本味，制作精细。

        The raw materials pay attention to the variety and season, in order to fully reflect the softness and crispness of the raw material texture. All seafood, fruits and vegetables used are based on the season, and poultry and livestock are mostly specialty products, fully reflecting the Zhejiang cuisine's emphasis on freshness in material selection and parts in material selection, following the principle of "four seasons in sequence".

原料讲究品种和季节时令，以充分体现原料质地的柔嫩与爽脆，所用海鲜、果蔬之品，无不以时令为上，所用家禽、畜类，均以特产为多，充分体现了浙菜选料讲究鲜活、用料讲究部位，遵循“四时之序”的选料原则。

        Zhejiang cuisine is renowned both domestically and internationally for its rich and diverse cooking techniques, with six specialties: stir frying, deep frying, stewing, stir frying, steaming, and roasting. The most important cooking method in Zhejiang cuisine is the method of cooking, which emphasizes the heat. There are more than 30 commonly used cooking methods, and due to the use of ingredients and techniques, emphasis is placed on the combination of main ingredients and flavors, resulting in a variety of flavors.

浙菜以烹调技法丰富多彩闻名于国内外，其中以炒、炸、烩、熘、蒸、烧6类为擅长。“熟物之法，最重火候”，浙菜常用的烹调方法有三十余类，因料施技，注重主配料味的配合，口味富有变化。

        The taste emphasizes freshness, crispness, and tenderness, while maintaining the natural color and true flavor of the ingredients. The form of Zhejiang cuisine is exquisite, delicate, and elegant.

口味注重清鲜脆嫩，保持原料的本色和真味。
浙菜的菜品形态讲究，精巧细腻，清秀雅丽。

Zhejiang cuisine culture:
        Rich cultural color
        Many dishes in Zhejiang cuisine are full of beautiful legends and rich cultural connotations, which are a major feature of Zhejiang cuisine.

浙菜文化： 文化色彩浓郁
浙菜中许多菜肴都富有美丽的传说，文化色彩浓郁是浙江菜一大特色。

Zhejiang cuisine system
        Zhejiang, with a long history, has a rich variety of dishes, with small and exquisite dishes. The dishes are fresh, smooth, tender, crispy, soft, and refreshing, characterized by being clear, fragrant, crispy, tender, refreshing, and fresh. They play an important role in many local flavors in China. Zhejiang cuisine is mainly composed of four schools: Hangzhou, Ningbo, Shaoxing, and Wenzhou, each with strong local characteristics.

浙菜体系
具有悠久历史的浙江菜品种丰富，菜式小巧玲珑，菜品鲜美滑嫩、脆软清爽，其特点是清、香、脆、嫩、爽、鲜，在中国众多的地方风味中占有重要的地位。浙菜主要有杭州、宁波、绍兴、温州四个流派所组成，各自带有浓厚的地方特色。
